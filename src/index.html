Luto Mo, Itlog Mo!

Mechanics:

  1. All are allowed to participate.
  2. Note that the dish must already be cooked prior to the event.
  3. The criteria for judging are:
    • Usage of main ingredients (any edible egg/s) -50%
    • Facial expression during taste test -30%
    • Table setting/ presentation - 20%

Kanta Me!  Live Results

Mechanics:

  1. This activity will have 2 teams, composed of 5 members.
  2. A member is chosen to answer an item, all members must have the chance to answer.
  3. A music will be played and the contestant must identify the artist or singer, depending on the instruction.
  4. The first contestant to raise his/her hand will be given 3 seconds to answer. Please wait for your name to be called before answering. A point or points will awarded for each corre (Easy - 1 ; Med - 3 ; Hard - 5).
  5. If the first contestant to raise a hand is not able to answer, the song will be played again up to the very end if needed. At this point the other contestant need to answer the question within 3 secs
  6. The winning contestant will have a chance to add an extra point if he/she can sing part of the song and supply the missing lyrics.
  7. The team with highest score win.

Name It To Win It!  Live Results

Mechanics:

  1. All members will be need to participate.
  2. For every question answered or skipped, a new team member will answer the next question.
  3. You may coach your teammate, but he/she should say the final answer
  4. Each team will be given 1 minute for each category to answer the 10 questions
  5. When the buzzer sounds, the incomplete answer will not be counted

Paskuhan Na!

Mechanics:

  1. Each team should prepare a live or recorded CHRISTMAS CAROL.
  2. Note that live presentation will have extra points.
  3. The presentation should be only 3-5 minutes.
  4. The criteria for judging are:
    • Voice Quality - 40%
    • Creativity - 25%
    • Entertainment Value - 25%
    • Overall Impact - 10%
